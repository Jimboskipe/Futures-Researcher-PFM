Project 1: Six-Firm Comparison

Research & Methodology

Objective

Compare 6 major U.S. futures prop firms across comprehensive decision criteria to create a reference framework for trader evaluation.

Method

Analyzed 30+ decision criteria (fees, payouts, features, risk rules, support) through systematic research and sourced data. Built 50-cell comparison matrix with detailed notation.

Outcome

Production-ready comparison report and spreadsheet. Identified $4,080 price range across firms and documented key differentiators in risk rules and payout mechanics.
