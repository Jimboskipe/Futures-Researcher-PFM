PROP FIRM DATA ACCURACY AUDIT - FINDINGS MEMO
By: James M. Doyle  |  Date: 2026-07-08
1. WHAT I AUDITED
Compared PropFirmMatch listings against official firm websites and help centers for six futures prop firms (Apex, Topstep, MyFundedFutures, Take Profit Trader, Tradeify, Bulenox). Eight core fields per firm plus legacy/coverage checks.
2. RESEARCH METHODOLOGY (3 LOOPS)
* Loop 1 - PFM capture via WebFetch (curl blocked by Cloudflare).
* Loop 2 - Official verification against help centers and pricing pages.
* Loop 3 - Discrepancy hunt for stale Apex 4.0 content, missing SKUs, unlisted firms, misleading promos.
3. SUMMARY OF RESULTS
* Total checks: 50
* Matched (Yes): 28
* Differed (No): 13
* Unclear: 6
* N/A: 3
4. KEY DISCREPANCIES FOUND (ACTIONABLE)
* APEX - FAQ still lists REMOVED legacy rules (5:1 risk-reward, 30% consistency). Apex 4.0 uses 50% consistency and removed 5:1 RR.
* APEX - AI summary understates 50K EOD drawdown ($625-$1500 vs official $2000).
* APEX - AI summary lists account sizes up to $300K; new 4.0 purchases max $150K.
* APEX - Rules say EAs prohibited; Apex markets ProjectX API automation.
* MY FUNDED FUTURES - Core $77/mo plan NOT listed on PFM (catalog gap).
* TAKE PROFIT TRADER - Promo no activation fee is misleading vs $130 PRO activation unless credit applied post-pass.
* TOPSTEP - Page mixes Live DLL ($2000) with Combine rules ($1000 DLL on 50K).
* BULENOX - Firm UNLISTED on PropFirmMatch (coverage gap).
5. RECOMMENDATIONS
* Add last-verified dates and sweep Apex 4.0 legacy FAQ.
* Add MFFU Core SKU or publish deprecation note.
* Clarify TPT post-pass credit workflow in promo headlines.
* List or clearly mark unlisted firms like Bulenox.
