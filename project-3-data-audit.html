Project 3: PropFirmMatch Data Audit

By: James M. Doyle | Date: 2026-07-08

What I Audited

Compared PropFirmMatch listings against official firm websites and help centers for six futures prop firms (Apex, Topstep, MyFundedFutures, Take Profit Trader, Tradeify, Bulenox). Eight core fields per firm plus legacy/coverage checks.

Research Methodology (3 Loops)

  • Loop 1 — PFM capture via WebFetch (curl blocked by Cloudflare)
  • Loop 2 — Official verification against help centers and pricing pages
  • Loop 3 — Discrepancy hunt for stale Apex 4.0 content, missing SKUs, unlisted firms, misleading promos

3-Loop Verification Process

Each discrepancy was cross-checked through three independent sources: PropFirmMatch platform, official firm websites, and user-reported data from help centers. This ensures accuracy and identifies gaps in coverage.

Key Discrepancies Found (Actionable)

🚩 APEX

Issue: FAQ still lists REMOVED legacy rules (5:1 risk-reward, 30% consistency). Apex 4.0 uses 50% consistency and removed 5:1 RR.

Impact: New traders may be confused by outdated documentation.

🚩 APEX

Issue: AI summary understates 50K EOD drawdown ($625–$1500 vs official $2000).

Impact: Risk assessment inaccurate on comparison platform.

🚩 APEX

Issue: AI summary lists account sizes up to $300K; new 4.0 purchases max $150K.

Impact: Traders may select unavailable account sizes.

🚩 APEX

Issue: Rules say EAs prohibited; Apex markets ProjectX API automation.

Impact: Conflicting information on automation support.

🚩 MYFUNDEDFUTURES

Issue: Core $77/mo plan NOT listed on PFM (catalog gap).

Impact: Flagship budget product invisible on comparison site.

🚩 TAKE PROFIT TRADER

Issue: Promo "no activation fee" is misleading vs $130 PRO activation unless credit applied post-pass.

Impact: Traders confused about real cost structure.

🚩 TOPSTEP

Issue: Page mixes Live DLL ($2000) with Combine rules ($1000 DLL on 50K).

Impact: Risk parameters unclear per account type.

🚩 BULENOX

Issue: Firm UNLISTED on PropFirmMatch (coverage gap).

Impact: Major player missing from comparison platform.

Recommendations
